## Anime Overview

**Title:** Beat of Barriers  
**Genre:** Action, Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Adventure  
**Total Seasons:** 50  
**Average Episodes per Season:** 30  
**Average Chapters per Episode:** 3–5  

**Core Idea:**  
A multiverse-spanning story where heroes with unique abilities fight against villains controlling reality barriers. Powers evolve over time, armies interact dynamically, and the stakes escalate to a system-level threat.

## Story Structure

- **Episode Length:** ~30 min  
- **Chapters per Episode:** 3–5  
- **Season Arcs:** 3–5 mini-arcs per season

### Example: Season 1 – Awakening
1. Episodes 1–5: Introduce heroes, their abilities, and normal life.  
2. Episodes 6–10: First encounter with villain minions; small fight.  
3. Episodes 11–15: Hero unlocks new ability under threat.  
4. Episodes 16–20: Villain sets first barrier; heroes learn teamwork.  
5. Episodes 21–30: Finale; partial barrier breach, new threat revealed.

### Tips for Making Your Own Anime
- Start small: 3–4 main characters, 1 main villain.  
- Define a core theme: friendship, responsibility, growth.  
- Use “power progression”: weak → stronger over episodes.  
- Design villains with goals and strategy.  
- Plan arcs inside a big story for long-term engagement.  
- Visual powers uniquely; audiences remember visuals first.
